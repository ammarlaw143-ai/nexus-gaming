Our Story

NexusPlay was founded in 2021 by a small group of passionate gamers who were frustrated by scattered, hard-to-find gaming information. We wanted one place that had everything — game listings, honest reviews, expert guides, and breaking news.

Today, NexusPlay serves over 5 million gamers monthly across 50+ countries. We remain independent, ad-supported, and committed to providing the best free gaming content on the internet.

🎯

Our Mission

To make gaming discovery, learning, and news accessible to every gamer in the world — completely free, without paywalls or subscriptions.

🛡️

Our Values

Honesty, transparency, and independence. We never accept payment for reviews. Every score and recommendation is earned, never bought.

🌍

Our Community

Gamers from over 50 countries trust NexusPlay daily. We're building a global community united by a shared love of gaming.
